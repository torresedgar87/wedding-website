How we met

We met on a summer calculus class back in our local community college. In fact, Edgar was not enrolled in the class. He was hoping to "crash" the class and, out of 10+ people, he got picked by random to join the class! The rest is history. Thank you universe because otherwise we wouldn't be here! (:

The proposal

We love San Diego and Balboa Park has always been a special place for us. Our proposal took place at the Japanese Friendship Garden. Edgar surprised Mariapaz by "capturing the moment", followed by an impromptu photo session and ended with an intimate surprise gathering with our immediate family.

Event Schedule

Arrival and Seating 6:00PM - 6:30PM

Upon arriving at the lower garden entrance we ask our guest to follow signs to the ceremony location. There will be a water station available so please feel free to grab a drink if needed. The first few seated rows will be reserved for family and will be marked as such. All other guest please feel free to sit where available. Please avoid leaving single seats open so that everyone may have a place to sit. Once seated we ask you avoid getting up unless necessary. We want to start the ceremony promptly so we ask for your cooperation.

Ceremony 6:30PM - 7:00pm

We are excited to have everyone be part of our ceremony and ask for everyones cooperation so we can start the ceremony promptly. We ask everyone to please silence any phones, refrain from flash photography, loud conversation, and to please remain seated at all times except when appropriate to stand. We hired a really great photographer and we would love for her to be able to capture all the amazing moments.

Cocktail Hour 7:00PM - 10:00PM

After the ceremony we would like to invite all our guest to join us for a extended happy hour in lieu of a reception. There will be appetizers/food stations, drinks, and desserts. We encourage everyone to grab a drink and take a walk around the beautiful garden.
