How we met

It started out like all great romances do, on a warm summer day... studying calculus at our community college library! Edgar met Mariapaz in summer school, in the summer of 2009. Fun fact: Edgar wasn’t actually enrolled in the class. On the first day of class, he was picked from out of 10+ people by random to join. Little did they know that day would change the rest of their lives. They eventually met and got to know each other during study group sessions. After summer school ended, they stayed in contact and started dating later that year.

The proposal

Ever since they graduated from college, Edgar and Mariapaz have always dreamt of a life in San Diego. For this reason, Balboa Park in San Diego has always been a special place in their hearts. In October of 2018, Edgar tricked Mariapaz into thinking they were just going to go on a date at the Japanese Friendship Garden in Balboa Park. Edgar hired a photographer to follow them around and capture the moment. After Edgar surprised Mariapaz with the proposal, they had an impromptu photoshoot at the garden. They ended the evening with a surprise gathering with their immediate families.

Garden Rules

Any damage done to the garden must be paid by the bride and groom so please be respectful of the garden's plants, trees, and wildlife. Observe all signs. Stay on designated paths when walking the garden. Do not remove any property or plants. Smoking is prohibitted inside the garden. Do not feed the koi fish or throw any objects in the water. Do not litter. Dispose of trash only at designated areas.

Fully vaccinated proof

Vaccination card (which includes name of person vaccinated, type of vaccine provided and date last dose administered) OR a photo of a vaccination card as a separate document OR a photo of the attendee's vaccine card stored on a phone or electronic device OR documentation of vaccination from a healthcare provider.

California: Please visit Digital Vaccine Record to receive a digital copy of your vaccination record.

Negative test proof

Printed document (from the test provider or laboratory) OR an email or text message displayed on a phone from the test provider or laboratory. The information should include person's name, type of test performed, and negative test result (date of test must be within prior 72 hours).
